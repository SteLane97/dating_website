Our Global Reach

Discover how Connecting Hearts is bringing people together all over the world.

Global Presence

Connecting Hearts has experienced significant growth since its founding and now serves users across multiple continents. Our platform is designed to provide efficient access to our services, ensuring that people from around the world can easily find their match.

Global Statistics
Active in 25+ countries
Over 2 million registered users worldwide
15,000+ successful matches each month
Available in 8 languages

Europe

Our European presence is centered in London, with a strong user base throughout the United Kingdom. We also have thriving communities in Paris, Berlin, Madrid, Rome, and other major European cities. European users appreciate our focus on meaningful connections and our verification process.

North America

Connecting Hearts has a strong presence in the United States, with particularly active communities in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, and Miami. We also have growing user bases in Canada, particularly in Toronto and Vancouver. North American users value our privacy features and diverse community.

Asia-Pacific

Our Asia-Pacific presence is anchored by our active community in Tokyo, with growing user bases in Sydney, Singapore, Seoul, and Auckland. This region has shown the fastest growth in the past year, with a 45% increase in active users. The Asia-Pacific market appreciates our culturally sensitive approach and multilingual support.

South America

Led by our community in Rio de Janeiro, our South American presence has been steadily growing. We also have active users in São Paulo, Buenos Aires, Lima, and Bogotá. South American users particularly enjoy our event features and community-building initiatives.

Local Events and Initiatives

As part of our commitment to building communities, we organize regular events in major cities around the world. These events include:

  • Speed Dating Events in London, New York, and Tokyo
  • Relationship Workshops in Paris and Sydney
  • Community Mixers in Berlin and Rio de Janeiro
  • Charity Fundraisers in Toronto and Singapore

These events provide opportunities for our users to connect in person, enhancing their online dating experience with real-world interactions.

International Growth Strategy

Our approach to global expansion is guided by these key principles:

  1. Cultural Adaptation - We tailor our platform and features to respect local dating norms and preferences.
  2. Language Support - We continually add new languages to make our platform accessible to more users.
  3. Local Partnerships - We collaborate with local organizations to better understand and serve each market.
  4. Community Building - We foster a sense of community through local events and initiatives.
  5. Continuous Improvement - We gather feedback from users around the world to enhance our global service.
