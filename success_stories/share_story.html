Share Your Story

Tell us how you found love on Connecting Hearts and inspire others on their dating journey.

Why Share Your Story?

Your story can inspire others who are looking for meaningful connections. By sharing your experience, you help create a community of hope and encouragement for those who are still on their dating journey.

Selected stories may be featured on our website, social media, or marketing materials. You might even be chosen for our Featured Couples section, where we share in-depth stories of relationships that began on Connecting Hearts.

What Happens Next?

After you submit your story, here's what you can expect:

  1. Confirmation - You'll receive an email confirming that we've received your submission.
  2. Review - Our team will review your story within 14 days.
  3. Publication - If selected for our testimonials page, your story will be published within 30 days.
  4. Featured Couple - If selected for our Featured Couples section, we'll contact you to schedule an interview and possibly a photo session.

Thank you for sharing your love story with us! Your experience may inspire someone else to find their perfect match on Connecting Hearts.
