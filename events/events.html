Event Guidelines

  1. Registration - Most events require advance registration due to limited capacity. Please register at least 48 hours before the event.
  2. Cancellations - If you need to cancel, please do so at least 24 hours in advance to allow others to join.
  3. Online Events - Access details for online events will be sent to your registered email 1 hour before the event starts.
  4. Code of Conduct - All participants are expected to behave respectfully. Inappropriate behavior will result in removal from the event and possibly from the platform.
