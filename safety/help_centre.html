Frequently Asked Questions

Account Management

How do I create an account?

To create a Connecting Hearts account:

  1. Visit our homepage and click "Create Your Profile" or "Sign Up"
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Provide basic information (name, date of birth, gender, etc.)
  4. Verify your email address by clicking the link in our confirmation email
  5. Complete your profile with photos and personal details

Remember, a complete profile increases your chances of finding compatible matches!

How do I edit my profile?

To edit your profile:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on your profile picture or username in the top right corner
  3. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu
  4. Update your information in the relevant sections
  5. Click "Save Changes" when you're done

You can edit your profile as often as you like to keep it updated and relevant.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact our support team for assistance.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  4. Follow the prompts to confirm deletion

Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed from our active systems within 30 days, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.

Subscriptions and Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay (on iOS devices)
  • Google Pay (on Android devices)
  • Direct bank transfers (for annual subscriptions only)

All payments are processed securely through our payment processors.

How do I upgrade to a premium membership?

To upgrade to a premium membership:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Upgrade" in the top navigation bar or visit your Account Settings
  3. Select the membership plan that suits your needs
  4. Choose your preferred billing cycle (monthly, 3-month, 6-month, or annual)
  5. Enter your payment details and confirm your subscription

Your premium features will be activated immediately after payment confirmation.

How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Go to "Account Settings"
  3. Select "Subscription" or "Billing"
  4. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm cancellation

After cancellation, your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period. We don't offer prorated refunds for unused subscription time.

Can I get a refund?

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • If you encounter technical issues that prevent you from using our service, you may be eligible for a refund. Contact our support team with details of the problem.
  • If you accidentally purchased a subscription or made a duplicate payment, contact us within 7 days for assistance.
  • We do not offer refunds for change of mind, lack of matches, or other non-technical issues.

Each refund request is evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team to discuss your specific situation.

Using the Platform

How does the matching algorithm work?

Our matching algorithm analyzes multiple factors to suggest compatible matches, including:

  • Your stated preferences and deal-breakers
  • Personality traits and values
  • Mutual interests and lifestyle choices
  • Relationship goals and expectations
  • Location and distance preferences

The algorithm learns from your interactions over time, improving its recommendations based on the profiles you engage with. To get the best matches, we recommend completing your profile thoroughly and keeping your preferences updated.

How do I message other users?

To message other users:

  1. Navigate to the profile of the person you want to contact
  2. Click the "Message" button on their profile
  3. Type your message in the text box
  4. Click "Send"

Please note that messaging is a premium feature. Free users can respond to messages they receive from premium members, but cannot initiate conversations. Upgrade to a premium membership to unlock unlimited messaging.

How do I use the Video Chat feature?

To use the Video Chat feature (available to premium members):

  1. Open a conversation with the person you want to video chat with
  2. Click the video camera icon in the top right of the conversation window
  3. The other person will receive a video chat request notification
  4. Once they accept, the video call will begin

Both users must have granted camera and microphone permissions to their browser for the video chat to work properly. For privacy reasons, users must explicitly accept video chat requests before a call can connect.

How do I block or report a user?

To block a user:

  1. Visit their profile
  2. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Block User" from the dropdown menu

To report inappropriate behavior:

  1. Visit the user's profile or open your conversation with them
  2. Click the three dots (⋮) in the top right corner
  3. Select "Report User"
  4. Choose the reason for reporting and provide any additional details
  5. Submit the report

Our safety team reviews all reports promptly and takes appropriate action according to our community guidelines.

Common Issues

I'm not receiving any matches

If you're not receiving matches, consider these potential solutions:

  • Complete your profile - Profiles with photos and detailed information receive more matches
  • Broaden your preferences - Very narrow preferences may limit your potential matches
  • Check your location settings - Ensure your location is set correctly and consider expanding your distance range
  • Regular activity - Using the platform regularly signals that you're an active user

If you've tried these steps and still aren't receiving matches, contact our support team for assistance.

I can't upload photos

If you're having trouble uploading photos, try these solutions:

  • Check that your photos meet our requirements: JPG or PNG format, at least 200x200 pixels, and less than 5MB in size
  • Ensure you have a strong internet connection
  • Try a different browser or device
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Make sure you're using the latest version of your browser

If you continue to experience issues, please contact our support team with details about the problem and the device you're using.

The app or website is running slowly

If you're experiencing performance issues, try these steps:

  • Check your internet connection speed and stability
  • Close other applications or browser tabs that might be using your bandwidth
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Update your browser to the latest version
  • Restart the app or your device

If the problem persists, please contact our support team with details about the issue, including your device type, browser version, and a description of the slowness you're experiencing.
