Privacy Policy

Understanding how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

At Connecting Hearts, we value the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and safeguarding of your data when you use our website and services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand how we handle your information. By using Connecting Hearts, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

Privacy Policy Summary

  • We collect personal information that you provide when creating an account, completing your profile, and using our services.
  • We use your information to provide, improve, and personalize our services, including matching you with compatible users.
  • We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • You have control over your data, including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.
  • You can contact our Data Protection Officer with any privacy-related questions or concerns.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our platform:

Information You Provide to Us:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your name, email address, date of birth, gender, and sexual orientation.
  • Profile Information: Information you add to your profile such as photos, interests, relationship preferences, lifestyle details, and personal descriptions.
  • Communication Information: Messages you exchange with other users through our platform.
  • Survey Information: If you participate in surveys or provide feedback, we collect your responses.
  • Payment Information: If you subscribe to premium services, we collect payment details (processed securely through our payment processors).
  • Support Information: Information you provide when contacting our customer support team.

Information We Collect Automatically:

  • Usage Information: How you interact with our platform, including features you use, time spent on the site, and browsing patterns.
  • Device Information: Type of device, operating system, browser type, and IP address.
  • Location Information: General location based on IP address or more precise location if you grant permission.
  • Log Information: Browser type, access times, pages viewed, and system activity.

Information From Third Parties:

  • Social Media Information: If you choose to link your social media accounts, we may receive information from those platforms.
  • Verification Information: We may receive information from identity verification services if you use our verification features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing Our Services: To create and manage your account, operate our platform, and deliver the services you request.
  • Matching: To suggest compatible matches based on your preferences and profile information.
  • Communication: To enable communication between users and to send you service-related notifications.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and resolve issues.
  • Improvement and Analysis: To analyze usage patterns, develop new features, and improve our services.
  • Safety and Security: To verify accounts, prevent fraud, and enhance user safety.
  • Marketing: To send you information about our services, promotions, and events (you can opt out of marketing communications).
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

The legal bases for processing your information include:

  • Performing our contract with you
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Our legitimate interests (such as improving our services and ensuring safety)
  • Your consent, where applicable

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Information in your profile is visible to other users according to your privacy settings. Messages you send are visible to the recipients.
  • Service Providers: We share information with trusted third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as hosting, data analysis, payment processing, and customer service.
  • Business Transfers: If Connecting Hearts is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Safety and Protection: We may share information to protect the safety of our users, our company, or others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where:

  • We need to retain the information to comply with legal obligations
  • The information is necessary for the establishment, exercise, or defense of legal claims
  • The information needs to be maintained for legitimate business interests, such as fraud prevention

Certain information may remain in our backup systems for a period of time after deletion from our active systems.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can update or correct inaccurate information through your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and associated personal information at any time.
  • Objection: You can object to certain processing of your information.
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict processing of your information in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Consent Withdrawal: Where we process data based on consent, you can withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@connectinghearts.com.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority if you are concerned about our handling of your personal information.

6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activities and to distinguish you from other users. Cookies help us provide you with a better experience and allow us to improve our site.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the operation of our platform.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Allow us to recognize and count visitors and analyze how users navigate our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: Used to recognize you when you return to our site.
  • Targeting Cookies: Record your visit to our site, the pages you have visited, and the links you have followed.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, blocking certain cookies may impact your experience and the services we offer.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, accidental loss, alteration, or destruction. Some of our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Incident response plans

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. International Data Transfers

Connecting Hearts operates globally, which means your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. These countries may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

When we transfer personal information outside of the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, we use appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission or UK authorities, as applicable.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we take steps to remove such information and terminate the child's account.

If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child, please contact us at privacy@connectinghearts.com.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. In some cases, we may also notify you through email or platform notifications.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
